Another batch of changes in RoundsBoard:

- **Organizations** — create, edit and manage organizations yourself, and assign them to tournaments.
- **Organizer management** — tournament organizer roles and inviting co-organizers by e-mail.
- **Account sign-up** — you can now create an organizer account in RoundsBoard yourself.
- **Tournament location editing** — automatically place the tournament on the map from its address, or set it manually (no more typing in coordinates by hand!).
